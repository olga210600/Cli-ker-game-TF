Personal Details

Many years ago, people in the Tungwali settlement lived in peace and harmony, farming and other crafts. The tribe was ruled for many years by Chief Bari. He was a wise and just chief, and during his reign the people became united and began to learn new trades. The chief had two sons, the elder Duran and the younger Aaron. Duran was an excellent hunter, and Aaron helped his father in everything, he spent much time with the people of their settlement and helped them.

Many years passed by, the old Bari felt his imminent death, and decided to hand over the reign to his youngest son, for he saw in him a good leader who would take care of his people.

Soon after, Bari died, and the eldest son, learning of his father's decision, revolted and decided to kill his younger brother Aaron in order to seize power.
